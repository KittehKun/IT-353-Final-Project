Welcome to Profilizer!

What is profilizer?

Profilizer is an application where users are able to sign up to the website and foster their own online profile! Users will have the ability to create their own online profile where others will be able to search for your profile.

What are the features?

Profilizer hosts a ton of features to get your online profile started. Here is a list of everything Profilizer offers to you!

  • Create and fully customize your profile
  • Upload ONE profile front-page video that will be showed to all upon search
  • Friend and Chat other users
  • Search for other Profilizer users
  • And many more to come...

How do I get started?

It's simple! You can click the button below to get your profile created. Our application process is designed to be as quick as possible so you can spend more time showing off your cool profile to your friends and fellow Profilizer users!
